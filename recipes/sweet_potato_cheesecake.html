Sweet Potato Cheesecake

Description

This sweet potato cheesecake has a smooth, creamy texture with a buttery crumb crust and delectable pecan praline topping. Perfect for a rich and flavorful fall or winter dessert.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 3 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 (15 ounce) can sweet potato puree
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 cup chopped pecans
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup heavy whipping cream

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Mix graham cracker crumbs and melted butter in a bowl until evenly moistened. Press mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan to form the crust.

  2. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Mix in sweet potato puree, milk, cinnamon, and nutmeg until well combined. Pour filling over crust in springform pan.

  3. Bake in preheated oven for 60 minutes, or until center is almost set. Remove from oven and let cool.

  4. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine chopped pecans, brown sugar, butter, and heavy whipping cream. Cook and stir until mixture comes to a boil. Boil for 2 minutes, then remove from heat.

  5. Pour pecan topping over cooled cheesecake. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.
