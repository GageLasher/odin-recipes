Mini Brownie Turkeys

Description

These adorable mini brownie turkeys are perfect for Thanksgiving or any fall gathering. Made with rich brownies, colorful frosting, and candy decorations, they are sure to delight both kids and adults alike.

Ingredients

  • 1 box brownie mix (plus ingredients called for on the box)
  • 1 cup chocolate frosting
  • 1 cup candy corn
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup red and orange M&M's
  • 1/4 cup white frosting
  • Black decorating gel

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven according to brownie mix instructions. Prepare brownie mix as directed on the box. Pour batter into a greased 9x13 inch baking pan and bake as directed. Allow brownies to cool completely.

  2. Once cooled, cut brownies into small squares (about 2 inches). These will be the bodies of the turkeys.

  3. Spread a generous amount of chocolate frosting on top of each brownie square.

  4. Insert candy corn pieces around the back edge of each brownie to create the turkey's feathers.

  5. Use mini chocolate chips for the eyes, placing them on the front of the brownie. Use red and orange M&M's for the beak and wattle, attaching them with a small dab of frosting.

  6. Use white frosting to create a small dot on each chocolate chip for added detail in the eyes. Use black decorating gel to add pupils to the eyes.

  7. Let the frosting set for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy your mini brownie turkeys!
